Chaat is a family of savory snacks that orginated in india and typically served at roadside tracks from stalls or food carts. Chaats are traditional indian street foods made with layers of bold flavours and textures for a result thta is very lickable. Chaat is an umbrells term for a wide range of roadside foods thta usually features some kind of fried dough with various ingredients that typically create a spicy and tangy flavour.
